THE LAST
WITNESS

A new premise for discussion. This is a strict tree: every card has one parent, except the root. No foldbacks, no shared outcomes.

Edric killed Thomas—Garrick’s brother—when Thomas tried to ring Greywatch’s eastern alarm. Edric claims the bell would have exposed families hiding beneath the road. Garrick wants him dead. Mara needs him alive: Edric is the last lantern keeper who can reach the Vault, decode its ledger, and warn the inner wall before it falls.
